Abyss Dungeon 2D

May, 2024

Abyss Dungeon 2D is a retro-style dungeon crawler that will test your courage and reflexes! Traverse through perilous dungeons, dodging traps, solving tricky puzzles, and slaying fearsome enemies, all while hunting for hidden treasures. Equip powerful weapons, brew potions in the alchemy pot, and get lost in a world of stunning 2D artwork. With each step, the dungeon changes, keeping you on your toes with procedurally generated levels.

List of Features:

  • 🧪 Alchemy System: Collect item drops from defeated enemies and brew potions to enhance your abilities using the alchemy pot.

  • 🎒 Inventory Management: Organize your loot with an easy-to-use inventory system. Drag and drop items with the mouse, and right-click to divide stacks.

  • 🏰 Procedural Dungeon Generation: Every playthrough is unique! Dungeons are randomly generated, offering new layouts, challenges, and secrets each time you dive in.

  • 🎨 Pixel Art with Aseprite: Beautiful 2D pixel art and animations were crafted using Aseprite, giving the game a retro yet polished look.

  • ⚙️ Built with Unity: Developed using Unity, ensuring smooth gameplay, responsive controls, and a solid game experience across different devices.

  • 🐉 Challenging Bosses and Enemies: Face off against a variety of creatures, each requiring different strategies to defeat.

  • 🎵 Atmospheric Soundtrack: Immerse yourself in the dungeon with a captivating soundtrack designed to enhance the game’s eerie atmosphere

Sudoku 2D

April, 2024

🧩 Sudoku 2D brings a fresh twist to the classic number puzzle, combining slick visuals with smooth gameplay for endless brain-teasing fun. Whether you’re a seasoned Sudoku solver or just getting into the game, Sudoku 2D delivers the perfect balance of challenge and satisfaction. With vibrant colors, intuitive design, and dynamically generated puzzles, it’s Sudoku like you’ve never seen before!

List of features:

  • 🔢 Dynamic Puzzle Generation: Each puzzle is uniquely generated, offering endless variety and no repeats.

  • 💡 Hint System: Stuck on a tough spot? Hints are just a tap away to keep you moving forward.

  • ⏪ Undo Function: Made a mistake? No worries—use the undo button to easily fix your errors.

  • 🎨 Minimalist Design: Clean, modern visuals that put the focus on solving puzzles without distractions.

  • 🌈 Vibrant Animations: Enjoy snazzy animations and a colorful interface as you fill in those numbers.

  • 🧘‍♂️ Multiple Difficulty Levels: Ranging from beginner-friendly to expert-level, there’s a challenge for every player.
